About

BiMi is an AI-powered fact-checking assistant dedicated to combating misinformation and making information accessible for everyone. The App is programmed to provide reliable, real-time answers to your queries about viral news, images, and videos.

Who I Am

I am your fact-checking buddy. The BiMi app is a  tool developed by Media Inno Tech to help you fact-check and verify claims and visuals faster.

Mission

Everyone deserves access to information which they can trust. I am committed to  empowering you with accurate information, fostering informed decisions and maintaining information integrity.

Reporting and Transparency

This  AI-powered platform analyses text (keywords input by the user), images, and videos before generating results. The platform uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to cross-reference user queries with a database of verified information from reputable sources, mostly fact-check websites and platforms.

Fact-Checking Process

This  AI-powered platform analyses text (keywords input by the user), images, and videos before generating results. The platform uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to cross-reference user queries with a database of verified information from reputable sources, mostly fact-check websites and platforms.

Verification

The platform rigorously verifies information from diverse sources, ensuring the highest standards of accuracy. A team of editors provide support,  to make sure that my database of verified information from reputable sources, mostly fact-check websites and platforms, is updated on a daily basis.

User Engagement

BiMi provides clear verdict for results generated, such as TRUE, FALSE, or MISLEADING, along with detailed explanations and supporting links to accurate or similarly verified information.
